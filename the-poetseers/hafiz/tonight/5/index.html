The Subject Tonight is Love

The Subject Tonight is Love

Wild and Sweet Poems of Hafiz

by D. Ladinsky

“Know

The true nature of your Beloved.

In His loving eyes your every thought,

Word and movement is always-

Always Beautiful.”

- Hafiz
