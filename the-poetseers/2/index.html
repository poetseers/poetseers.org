The Poet Seers

From the ancient cultures of the East to the present time, the great Poet Seers inspire and illumine humanity to look beyond the mundane and to gain a glimpse of the Beyond.

A Selection of Poet Seers

Homer (850 BC) William Blake (1757 – 1827)
Lord Buddha (560 BC) William Wordsworth (1770 – 1850)
Lao Tzu (c 500 BC) John Keats (1795 – 1821)
Rumi (1207 – 1273) Ralph Waldo Emerson (1803 – 1882)
Dante Alighieri (1265 – 1321) Walt Whitman (1819 – 1892)
Hafiz (1325 – 1389) Emily Dickinson (1830 – 1886)
Kabir (1440 – 1518) Rabindranath Tagore (1861 – 1941)
William Shakespeare (1564 – 1616) Sri Aurobindo (1872 – 1950)
Milton (1608 – 1674) Sri Chinmoy (1931 – 2007)
