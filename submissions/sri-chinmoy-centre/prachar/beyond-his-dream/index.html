Beyond His Dream

You wore the Crown of the Supreme.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You became the lion, king of animals.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You became the greatest warrior of Your time.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You became the mightiest emperor of the dynasty.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You conquered death, time and space.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You became the yogi of all yogis.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You rose beyond the cosmic Gods.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You steered the boat of history
          and altered universal destiny.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You became one with the Cosmic Heart.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You redefined aspiration, realisation
          and perfection.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You transcended the creative output of humanity.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You signalled the rout of the hostile forces.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You fashioned Your own extra-celestial Loka.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You erased the horizons of universal Love.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You planted the time-bomb of Supreme’s
          Compassion-Victory.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You brought mankind face to face
          With God’s Hour.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You enslaved Yourself to Your childrens’ whim.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You drank the entire sea of earth-sufferings.
     Still, You were not satisfied.
You carried Your Supreme
          Beyond His Dream
     Through the consecrated Sacrifice
               Of Your Satisfaction.

by: Prachar

- Sri Chinmoy Centre Poetry
