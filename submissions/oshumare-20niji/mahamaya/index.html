Mahamaya

The Dance of matter is a strange illusion

distracting us from seeing the whole truth

ephemeral, yet billed as ‘absolute’

immersed within this false reality

we fail to grasp the nature of the Great

and shield ourselves far from the Ultimate.

We’re taught we cannot touch the Ultimate

we’re taught to think our dreams are an illusion

that power and wealth are only for the great

that Magic has no basis in the truth

that loneliness is stark reality

and in our arrogance, we’re absolute.

We’re starting to see through that ‘absolute’

the walls between us and the Ultimate

are thinning, and a door of Reality

is opening up within our mass illusion

Science and Spirituality are both truth

and one without the other is less than great.

And this is what makes quantum physics great

there are no limits to the Absolute

what one desires enough becomes the truth

influencing the flow of the Ultimate

both space and time are really an illusion

we co-create our own reality.

And Maya, too, has Her Reality

Her metaphoric Dance of small and great

invites us to make sense of Her Illusion

teaching that LOVE IS THE ONLY ABSOLUTE

whatever your name is for the Ultimate

there can be no exception to this truth.

COMPASSION IS THE MULTIVERSAL TRUTH

and UNITY IS TRUE REALITY

empowering us to embrace the Ultimate

if we make sure our Dreams are truly Great

we’ll get a foretaste of the Absolute

a peek behind Her sari of Illusion.

The ABSOLUTE REALITY of Life?

Separation is a GREAT ILLUSION–

ONENESS IN LOVE IS THE ULTIMATE TRUTH!
