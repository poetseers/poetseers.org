Poems about Jesus Christ

by Sri Chinmoy

Jesus gave the world
His promise-sky.
The Christ gave the world
His aspiration-sun.

——-

Jesus died on the cross
To bear the punishment for your sins.
The Christ lives in your heart-cave
To enjoy the purity of your soulful prayers.

——-

Jesus Christ’s Sermon on the Mount
Is Eternity’s
Unparalleled source of inspiration
For the aspiring humanity.
Mother Earth cried through his heart.
Father Heaven smiled through his eyes.       

——-

Jesus came to you quickly
To love you
When you were young.
The Christ has come to you unconditionally
To save you
When you are old.

——-

Jesus,
Do show me
The God-seeker
Who is crying for You.

Christ,
Do show me
The God-lover
Who is smiling at You.

——-

Although Jesus has promised
To come back,
We shall not see Jesus.
But the Christ we shall see
Face to face.

——-

Come to Me O Christ

“O Christ – Thou rarest flower of hearts – Thou didst sail on the storm-tossed lake of prejudiced minds. Its evil-scented, gloomy thought-waves lashed Thy lily-tender soul. They crucified Thee with their evil. Yet Thou didst shed the aroma of goodness and forgiveness, and didst help them to be purified by remorse, so helping them to become attractively sweet-scented with Thine all-loving Flower-Soul. “

Come to Me O Christ by Paramahansa Yogananda from: Whispers of Eternity
