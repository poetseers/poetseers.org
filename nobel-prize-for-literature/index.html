Nobel Prize Poets

Recipients of the Nobel Prize for Literature PoetsInclude:

  • Rabindranath Tagore (1913)
  • W.B. Yeats (1923)
  • Gabriela Mistral(1945)
  • T.S. Eliot (1948)
  • Pablo Neruda (1971)
  • Czeslaw Milosz (1980)
  • Octavia Paz (1990)
  • Seamus Heaney (1995)
  • Wislawa Szymborska (1996)
  • Rudyard Kipling (1907)
  • Romain Rolland

Since 1901, the Nobel Prize has been awarded annually as per Alfred Nobel’s last will and testament to people who have been nominated for their commitment and vision.
