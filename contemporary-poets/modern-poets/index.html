Modern Poets is devoted particularly to the generation of poets whose work developed from the 1900s through the 1940s, including Wallace Stevens, Marianne Moore, Hart Crane, Robert Frost, W. H. Auden, Mina Loy, Langston Hughes, and William Carlos Williams. Poets whose careers were briefer or less influential are also included as well as later poets who have brought modernist poetics into the contemporary scene.
