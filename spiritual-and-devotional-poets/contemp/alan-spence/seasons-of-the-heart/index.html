Seasons of the Heart

First warmth of spring
I feel as if
I have been asleep

first warmth of spring
under cracking ice
the jawbone of a dog

crocuses
where last week
the snow lay thick

the spring breeze -
the paper flowers also
tremble

into the sea I launch
a piece of driftwood –
with great ceremony!

spring rain
a yellow oil-drum
bobbing down the river

By: Alan Spence
