Hazrat Inayat Khan

Prayers of Hazrat Inayat Khan

Invocation

Toward the One
the Perfection of
Love, Harmony and Beauty,
the Only Being,
United with all the illuminated Souls,
who form the Embodiment of the Master,
the Spirit of Guidance.

Amen

- Hazrat Inayat Khan
