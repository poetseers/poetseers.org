Truth alone triumphs

 

Transliteration

Satyam eva jayate.

 

Translation

Truth alone triumphs.

 

Comment

Truth is God’s Crown offered to God by God Himself.
Truth realised, God is forever caught.

 

Excerpt from: The Upanishads: The Crown Of India’s Soul

                    by: Sri Chinmoy.
