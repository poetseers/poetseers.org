The Goal Is Won

Sri Chinmoy has written over 1400 books including several hundred volumes of poetry and aphorisms which he has kindly given us permission to use.
We feel very grateful and fortunate to have access to this enormous resource and we shall endeavor to publish these works that not only span his life, but also convey the essence of his vision.
