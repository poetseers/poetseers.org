Stay Forever

Love, stay on earth
Forever,
Please.

Bliss, stay in Heaven
Forever,
Please.

Gratitude, stay in my heart
Forever,
Please.

Compassion, stay in God’s Heart
Forever,
Please.

From ‘ The Wings of Light ‘  by Sri Chinmoy
