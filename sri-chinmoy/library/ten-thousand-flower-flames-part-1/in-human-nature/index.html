In Human Nature

In human nature

There is something

Which everyone knows:

Bondage-bite.

In human nature

There is also something

Which practically nobody knows:

Freedom-kite.

- Sri Chinmoy

Excerpt from: Ten Thousand Flower-Flames, Part 1 by Sri Chinmoy, Copyright (c) 1900 by Sri Chinmoy – All rights reserved
