Tax-Exempt

Humility
Is exempt from fear-tax.
Nobility
Is exempt from jealousy-tax.
Generosity
Is exempt from doubt-tax.
Divinity
Is exempt from ignorance-tax.

By: Sri Chinmoy

From: The Dance of Life
